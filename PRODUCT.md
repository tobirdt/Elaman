# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Decision-makers, technical stakeholders, procurement teams, and operational specialists in governmental authorities, public-sector organisations, security-related organisations, and communication service providers. They visit in a professional context and need to establish credibility, scope, and a clear route to contact without encountering sales-funnel language or theatrical security imagery.

## Product Purpose

Present Elaman GmbH as an established German communications and security-engineering partner. The site must communicate trust, technical competence, long-standing experience, and full-service delivery in German and English, then make a direct business inquiry easy. Success means a qualified visitor can understand the company, its approach, and its relevance without unsupported claims or public operational detail.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exact, and assured. Institutional without bureaucracy; technical without spectacle; distinctive without fashion-led decoration. The emotional goal is earned confidence.

## Anti-references

- No glassmorphism, gradient washes, glow fields, cyberpunk, hacker imagery, surveillance-first hero language, bento layouts, icon-tile grids, or rounded feature-card walls.
- No editorial-magazine costume, decorative numbered section scaffolding, invented metrics, unsourced certifications, named clients, or implied secure-portal handling.
- No new palette hues, new fonts, or broad use of red outside protection semantics.
- Do not revive removed Scroll Story, DotMatrix, signal-diagram, glass-surface, or technical-grid alternatives.

## Design Principles

1. Make trust visible through hierarchy, restraint, and sourced facts rather than badges or claims.
2. Preserve the supplied heritage photography and verified content while giving each of the six sections a distinct, calm role.
3. Let the supplied points-only signet carry the header identity; do not repeat its geometry as decoration.
4. Give each viewport one dominant idea and make German copy a first-class composition constraint.
5. Keep motion limited to orientation and direct state feedback; static content remains complete without it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, keyboard access, focus visibility, semantics, and touch targets. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with immediate final states. Do not rely on colour alone, preserve readable document order, prevent horizontal overflow, and test both languages at mobile, tablet, and desktop widths.
